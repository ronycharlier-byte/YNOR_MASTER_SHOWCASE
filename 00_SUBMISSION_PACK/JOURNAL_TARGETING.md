## Short Editorial Pitch

This manuscript proposes a minimal, interpretation-neutral structural ontology for quantum reality. It combines an ontological framework, a formal sketch grounded in the standard quantum formalism, and a methodological principle (\(\mu\)) that selects ontologically admissible components by maximizing explanatory power while minimizing ontological cost and interpretative friction. The paper is positioned within structural realism and includes a reviewer-facing comparison with Everettian, Bohmian, QBist, and relational approaches.
